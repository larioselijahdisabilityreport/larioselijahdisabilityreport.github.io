What is Intellectual Disability?

Intellectual Disability is, according to AAIDD, "a disability characterized by significant limitations" (AAIDD).

Orci maecenas

Intellectual disability is a disability characterized by significant limitations. (#1 AAIDD) The most frequent types of intellectual disability we tend to see are autism, and down syndrome. A cause of intellectual disability is Genetic conditions. There can be a wrong code in the genes that gets passed on from parents, to give the child intellectual disability. A second cause for intellectual disability is Pregnancy Problems. The pregnant lady could have been under the influence while the baby was in her. And finally, Birth problems or after birth problems can also cause intellectual disability. Prematurely born children can get intellectual disability. So can children that get sick at an early age.
