How Intellectual Disabilities have challenged people
During the great depression and now

In the times of the Great Depression, people with intellectual disabilitys were being tormented by people without. They were also being told to kill themselves, which is also how people with other diseases also ended up. (#3 Munyi)
Doctors and other people working in the medical field recommended euthanasia for people with intellectual disability (#4 Harbour CK, Maulik PK.)
They overcame these challenges by having to try their best to do their best and not get in trouble and sent away or put down.
