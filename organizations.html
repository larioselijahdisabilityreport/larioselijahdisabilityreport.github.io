Organizations

Three organiations that help peoplewith intellectual disabilities

The titles of the organizations below are clickable, leading to their website.

  • The Arc

The Arc has been active for over 50 years and their purpose was to always make intelectual disabilities have better lives.

  • NCDJ

NCDJ helps by raising a lot of awareness for people with intellectual disabilities and making change.

  • AAIDD

AAIDD helps by, quoted from their websitte, "AAIDD promotes progressive policies, sound research, effective practices, and universal human rights for people with intellectual and developmental disabilities"
This means that they help the rights of people with intellectual disabilities and even does several other things while promoting rights of these people..
